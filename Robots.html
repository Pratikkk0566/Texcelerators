Our Journey

Texcelerators

From Concept to Champions

2023

Texcelerators Robotics Club was founded by passionate engineering students at SVPCET. Starting with just 5 members and a shared dream to innovate in robotics, we began our journey in a small corner of the college lab.

Early 2023

After months of learning and experimenting, we built our first functional robot - a simple line-following bot. Though basic, it marked our transition from theory to practical robotics and ignited our passion for innovation.

Mid 2023

Word spread about our innovative approach and dedication. Our team grew to 15+ members from different engineering branches, bringing diverse skills in mechanical design, electronics, programming, and project management.

Late 2023

We participated in our first inter-college robotics competition. Despite finishing 4th, the experience taught us valuable lessons about competition pressure, time management, and the importance of robust design.

Early 2024

Our breakthrough came when we won 1st place at the State Level Robotics Championship with our autonomous navigation robot. This victory put Texcelerators on the map and validated our innovative approach to robotics.

2024

Today, Texcelerators has 25+ active members, multiple award-winning robots, and partnerships with local industries. We're preparing for national competitions while mentoring the next generation of robotics enthusiasts.
