About Texcelerators

Our Mission

Texcelerators is a student-led robotics club dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology through innovation, collaboration, and competitive excellence. We believe in combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience to create cutting-edge robotic solutions.

Our Journey

Texcelerators

From Concept to Champions

2023

Texcelerators Robotics Club was founded by passionate engineering students at SVPCET. Starting with just 5 members and a shared dream to innovate in robotics, we began our journey in a small corner of the college lab.

Early 2023

After months of learning and experimenting, we built our first functional robot - a simple line-following bot. Though basic, it marked our transition from theory to practical robotics and ignited our passion for innovation.

Mid 2023

🏆 IIT Bombay Debut Victory : In our first-ever appearance at IIT Bombay’s Robotics Championship, we swept the podium with 1st, 2nd, and 3rd positions — earning national recognition and setting the tone for what Texcelerators stands for.

Late 2023

⚡️ Own Turf, Own Rules — Technex Takeover! : When Technex hit SVPCET, we turned up big. In the Coastal Clash showdown, we snatched all top 3 spots — 1st, 2nd, and 3rd — proving that when it's our ground, it's our game.

Early 2024

Our breakthrough came when we won 1st place at the State Level Robotics Championship with our autonomous navigation robot. This victory put Texcelerators on the map and validated our innovative approach to robotics.

2024

Today, Texcelerators has 25+ active members, multiple award-winning robots, and partnerships with local industries. We're preparing for national competitions while mentoring the next generation of robotics enthusiasts.

2024

🏅 Quark 2025 – A Win That Meant the World at BITS Goa After years of trying, learning, and coming close, we finally earned our place on the podium at BITS Goa’s Quark Festival. Securing 3rd place in Robo Sumo wasn’t just about ranking — it was about proving to ourselves that we’re capable. February 2025 gave us more than a medal — it gave us a moment we’ll carry forever.

2024

Today, Texcelerators has 25+ active members, multiple award-winning robots, and partnerships with local industries. We're preparing for national competitions while mentoring the next generation of robotics enthusiasts.
